NSERC’s Awards Database

NSERC’s Awards Database enables you to search the database containing all grants and scholarships awarded by NSERC since 1991. Once you choose one or more parameters, then conduct your search, a list of awards (or a report) will be displayed. You can then obtain more details on a specific project by clicking on it.
